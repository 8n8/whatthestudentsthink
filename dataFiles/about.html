What The Students Think

Simple charts of the 2017 National Student Survey

Purpose

To make it really easy to browse the results of the National Student Survey, and so to help with choosing undergraduate university courses in the UK.

Reasons for missing universities or subjects

Quoting from the notes that come with the data:

"The results are only publishable if there are at least 10 responses with a response rate of greater than or equal to 50%."

and

"...if no data is available for a particular course or institution where students were surveyed, the data did not meet publication thresholds. This does not reflect on the quality of the course or institution. It may be because the course is new or it is small, or we have not received enough survey responses."

In addition to this, I have excluded universities and courses that are very small.

First degree only

Only courses marked as "First degree" in the data are included. Other categories, such as "Other undergraduate", are excluded.

Part time courses are included

The charts include part time degree courses as well as full time ones.
